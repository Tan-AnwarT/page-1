Techniques

Master striking with our expert guides.

Step 1: Learn the Basics

Boxing Guards & Stances

  • Orthodox stance (left foot forward, right hand power)
  • Southpaw stance (right foot forward, left hand power)
  • Peek-a-boo guard
  • Philly Shell (shoulder roll)

Kickboxing Guards & Stances

  • Standard kickboxing stance
  • High guard
  • Long guard

Taekwondo Stances

  • Charyeot (attention stance)
  • Juchum Seogi (horse-riding stance)
  • Ap Seogi (walking stance)
  • Koa Seogi (cross stance)

Kyokushin Karate Stances

  • Zenkutsu Dachi (front stance)
  • Kiba Dachi (horse stance)
  • Sanchin Dachi (hourglass stance)

Step 2: Practice Combinations

Boxing Combos

  • 1-2 (jab → cross)
  • 1-2-3 (jab → cross → left hook)
  • 1-2-3-2 (jab → cross → hook → cross)

Kickboxing Combos

  • Jab → cross → left body kick
  • Cross → hook → low kick
  • Jab → right knee → hook

Taekwondo Combos

  • Jab → roundhouse
  • Double roundhouse → spinning back kick
  • Front kick → side kick

Kyokushin Karate Combos

  • Low kick → body punch → high kick
  • Front kick → reverse punch
  • Body hook → low kick → uppercut

Step 3: Focus on Footwork

  • Boxing: side steps, pivots, L-steps
  • Kickboxing: switch stance, circle away from power side
  • Taekwondo: bouncing movement, blitz steps
  • Kyokushin: rooted stance shifts

Step 4: Defense Techniques

  • Boxing: slips, rolls, parries
  • Kickboxing: leg checks, catching kicks
  • Taekwondo: high block, middle block
  • Kyokushin: absorb & counter

Step 5: Spar & Apply

Start with light sparring to test your skills. Increase intensity gradually.
